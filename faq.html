How do I book a service on HereForYou?

Booking a service is simple:

  1. Search for the service you need on our homepage
  2. Browse through verified professionals in your area
  3. Select a professional and view their profile
  4. Choose a convenient date and time slot
  5. Enter your address and service details
  6. Confirm your booking and payment method

You'll receive a booking confirmation with all details.

Can I cancel or reschedule my booking?

Yes, you can cancel or reschedule your booking:

  • Free cancellation: Up to 2 hours before the scheduled time
  • Rescheduling: Available up to 1 hour before the service
  • Late cancellation: May incur charges as per our policy

You can manage your bookings from the "My Bookings" section in your dashboard.

How far in advance can I book a service?

You can book services up to 30 days in advance. Same-day bookings are also available based on professional availability. For emergency services, we offer immediate booking with priority professionals.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept multiple payment methods for your convenience:

  • Digital Payments: UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), Credit/Debit Cards, Digital Wallets
  • Cash: Pay after service completion
  • Net Banking: All major banks supported

All digital payments are processed securely with bank-level encryption.

When do I need to pay for the service?

Payment timing depends on your chosen method:

  • Cash After Service: Pay the professional directly after work completion
  • Digital Payment: Payment is processed after service completion and your approval
  • Advance Booking: A small booking fee may be charged to confirm your slot

How do refunds work?

Our refund policy ensures customer satisfaction:

  • Service Issues: Full refund if service quality doesn't meet standards
  • Cancellations: Refunds based on cancellation timing and policy
  • Processing Time: 3-7 business days for digital payments
  • Cash Payments: Refunded via bank transfer

Are all professionals on HereForYou verified?

Yes, every professional goes through our comprehensive verification process:

  • Identity Verification: Aadhar and PAN card verification
  • Background Check: Police verification and reference checks
  • Skill Assessment: Practical tests and certifications
  • Training: Customer service and safety training
  • Insurance: All professionals are covered under our insurance policy

What if I'm not satisfied with the service?

Customer satisfaction is our priority. If you're not satisfied:

  1. Contact our support team immediately
  2. We'll investigate the issue within 24 hours
  3. Options include service redo, partial/full refund, or credit
  4. Serious issues result in professional review and training
  5. You can also rate and review to help other customers

How do you ensure service quality?

We maintain high quality through:

  • Regular Training: Ongoing skill development programs
  • Customer Feedback: Rating and review system
  • Quality Audits: Random quality checks
  • Professional Standards: Strict guidelines and code of conduct
  • Continuous Monitoring: Performance tracking and improvement

How do I create an account on HereForYou?

Creating an account is quick and easy:

  1. Click "Sign Up" on our homepage
  2. Enter your name, email, and phone number
  3. Verify your phone number with OTP
  4. Create a secure password
  5. Complete your profile with address details

You can also sign up using Google or Facebook for faster registration.

Can I have multiple addresses in my account?

Yes, you can save multiple addresses for convenience:

  • Home, office, and other locations
  • Quick selection during booking
  • Address-specific service preferences
  • Easy management from your profile settings

How do you ensure my safety during service visits?

Your safety is our top priority:

  • Professional Verification: Complete background verification
  • Real-time Tracking: Live location sharing during service
  • Emergency Support: 24/7 emergency helpline
  • Insurance Coverage: Comprehensive insurance for all services
  • Contact Masking: Privacy protection through masked communication
  • Rating System: Community-driven safety through reviews

What should I do in case of an emergency during service?

In case of any emergency:

  1. Immediate Help: Call our emergency number +91 9876543211
  2. App Emergency: Use the emergency button in the app
  3. Local Authorities: Call 100 for police or 108 for medical emergency
  4. Report Incident: Report to our support team for investigation

Our team is available 24/7 to assist you.

  • Major Cities: Kochi, Thiruvananthapuram, Thrissur, Kozhikode
  • Other Cities: Kollam, Alappuzha, Palakkad, Malappuram, Kannur, Kottayam
  • Expanding: We're constantly expanding to new areas

Can't find your city? Contact us - we might be launching there soon!
