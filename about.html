Our Story

2020

The Beginning

Founded in Kochi with a vision to solve the home services problem in Kerala. Started with just 50 verified professionals across 5 service categories.

2021

Rapid Growth

Expanded to Thiruvananthapuram and Thrissur. Reached 1,000+ professionals and served over 10,000 customers. Introduced our mobile app.

2022

State-wide Presence

Launched across all major cities in Kerala. Introduced advanced features like real-time tracking, instant booking, and 24/7 customer support.

2023

Innovation & Excellence

Launched AI-powered matching, introduced insurance coverage for all services, and achieved 4.8+ average rating across all services.

2024

Leading the Market

Now serving 100,000+ customers with 10,000+ verified professionals. Expanding to specialized services and enterprise solutions.
