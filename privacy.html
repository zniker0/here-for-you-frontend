At HereForYou Technologies Pvt Ltd ("we," "our," or "us"), we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform and services.

By using HereForYou, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with the practices described in this policy, please do not use our services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Register for an account
  • Book a service
  • Contact customer support
  • Participate in surveys or promotions
  • Apply to become a service professional

This information may include:

  • Identity Information: Full name, email address, phone number, date of birth
  • Address Information: Home address, service location, billing address
  • Payment Information: Payment method details, billing information
  • Professional Information: Skills, experience, certifications, documents
  • Profile Information: Profile picture, preferences, saved addresses

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect certain information when you use our platform:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser type, IP address
  • Usage Information: Pages visited, time spent, features used, search queries
  • Location Information: GPS coordinates, approximate location based on IP address
  • Communication Data: Messages, calls, and interactions on our platform

1.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information about you from:

  • Social media platforms (if you choose to sign up through them)
  • Payment processors
  • Background verification services
  • Other users (ratings, reviews, referrals)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

2.1 Service Provision

  • Create and maintain your account
  • Process and manage service bookings
  • Facilitate communication between users and professionals
  • Process payments and manage billing
  • Provide customer support

2.2 Safety and Security

  • Verify identity and conduct background checks
  • Monitor for fraudulent or suspicious activity
  • Ensure platform safety and security
  • Comply with legal obligations

2.3 Platform Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our services
  • Develop new features and functionality
  • Conduct research and analytics
  • Test and optimize user experience

2.4 Communication

  • Send booking confirmations and updates
  • Provide important service announcements
  • Send promotional offers (with your consent)
  • Respond to your inquiries and requests

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

3.1 With Service Professionals

When you book a service, we share necessary information with the assigned professional:

  • Your name and contact information
  • Service location and requirements
  • Booking details and preferences

3.2 With Other Users

  • Public profile information (name, ratings, reviews)
  • Service-related communications
  • Safety and verification information

3.3 With Service Providers

We work with third-party service providers who assist us in:

  • Payment processing
  • Background verification
  • Customer support
  • Analytics and marketing
  • Cloud storage and computing

3.4 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect our rights and property
  • Investigate fraud or security issues
  • Protect user safety and security

3.5 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity.

4. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your information:

4.1 Technical Safeguards

  • Encryption: Data encryption in transit and at rest
  • Access Controls: Limited access to authorized personnel only
  • Secure Infrastructure: Industry-standard security protocols
  • Regular Updates: Security patches and system updates

4.2 Organizational Safeguards

  • Employee training on data protection
  • Regular security audits and assessments
  • Incident response procedures
  • Confidentiality agreements with staff

4.3 Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. Inactive accounts may be deleted after 3 years of inactivity.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

5.1 Access and Portability

  • Request a copy of your personal information
  • Download your data in a portable format
  • View and update your profile information

5.2 Correction and Updates

  • Update your personal information anytime
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request verification of information accuracy

5.3 Deletion and Restriction

  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Restrict processing of your information
  • Object to certain uses of your data

5.4 Communication Preferences

  • Opt out of promotional communications
  • Choose notification preferences
  • Control marketing communications

How to Exercise Your Rights: Contact us at privacy@hereforyou.in or through your account settings to exercise any of these rights.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience:

6.1 Types of Cookies

  • Essential Cookies: Required for platform functionality
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how you use our service
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings
  • Marketing Cookies: Deliver relevant advertisements

6.2 Cookie Management

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling certain cookies may affect platform functionality.

6.3 Third-Party Tracking

We may use third-party analytics and advertising services that use their own tracking technologies.

7. Third-Party Services and Links

Our platform may contain links to third-party websites or integrate with third-party services:

  • Payment processors (Razorpay, PayU, etc.)
  • Social media platforms
  • Mapping and location services
  • Analytics providers

These third parties have their own privacy policies, and we are not responsible for their practices. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before using their services.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

If we discover that we have collected personal information from a child under 18, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

9. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. When we make changes:

  • We will post the updated policy on our website
  • We will update the "Last Updated" date
  • For significant changes, we will notify users via email or platform notifications
  • Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of the updated policy
